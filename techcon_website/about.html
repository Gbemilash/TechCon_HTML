Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local developer meetup. Over the years, it has evolved into a global event that unites thousands of professionals from across the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to promoting innovation, inclusivity, and the advancement of technology across all sectors. We aim to foster an open environment for collaboration and learning.
